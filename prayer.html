Guide to Praying for Church Ministries

Guide To Praying for Church Ministries:

Your Pastor

  • Pray for a powerful anointing of the Spirit on your pastor.
  • Pray for wisdom as your pastor attends to the administrative duties of the church.
  • Pray that your pastor will be refreshed in mind and spirit through the study of God’s Word and prayer.
  • Pray that your pastor will be blessed with good health.
  • Pray for God’s wisdom, guidance, and anointing upon your pastor in preparing messages each week.
  • Pray that your pastor will be undergirded by the Holy Spirit in times of loneliness or discouragement.
  • Pray that your pastor’s reputation in the community will be marked by respect and integrity.
  • Pray that your pastor’s heart and mind will be guarded against impure thoughts and deeds.
  • Pray that signs and wonders will follow the anointedproclamation of the Word.
    • li>Pray that your pastor’s relationships with family and friends will be strong and enriching.
  • Pray that your pastor will boldly proclaim truth, even if it’s unpopular.
  • Pray that good relationships will be built between your pastor and pastors of other churches in the community and district.
Prayer Topic #15

Prayer Topic

A Prayer for the Christian Education Department and R.E.A.L. Life Bible Studies

Scripture Reading: 2 Timothy 2:1-2, Acts 17:11

Reflections of the Heart:

May Christian Education always be R.E.A.L.- Relevant, Edifying, Accessible, Learning for the entire body. In addition, our desire is for Grow Groups to help WVCF grow in relationships with God and each other.

Scriptural Prayer:

Heavenly Father, we thank You for the covenant we have with You through Jesus Christ. Thank You for Your Word, which is a lamp to guide our feet and a light for our path. Thank You for giving us the Holy Spirit who guides us into all truth...

Father, among the ministry gifts, You have given us teachers who equip God’s people to do Your work and build up the church so that we will not be immature like children...

Personal Reflection:

Ask the Holy Spirit how you can become more involved in Christian Education.

Confession & Forgiveness:

Faithful Father, forgive us for not always seeking Your Kingdom above all else and sometimes failing to live righteously...

Closing Prayer:

Holy Spirit, help us all not to yield to temptation but deliver us from the evil one...

In Jesus’ name, Amen.
