What We Believe

Transparent Text Section

“WHAT’S THE DIFFERENCE?” Christians believe the Bible is the inspired Word of God, revealing His will and truth. It consists of the Old and New Testaments, guiding faith and practice. The Bible teaches about God’s love, salvation through Jesus Christ, and the promise of eternal life. It provides moral direction, wisdom, and encouragement for daily living. Believers trust it as authoritative and without error in its original manuscripts. Many view it as divinely preserved and relevant across generations. Through prayer and study, Christians seek to understand and apply its teachings, growing in faith and deepening their relationship with God.
